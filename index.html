Questions

Where can I pickup my order?

We are in Canada precisely at Terrebonne in Quebec province. If that works for you, place an order and you'll get the full address.

What type of payment do you accept?

We accept cash and interac e-transfer.

Do you offer deliver?

Yes, we offer delivery. Delivery costs vary according to your location and the quantity ordered. For a limited time, we offer free delivery on orders of $60 or more.

How long could I store your juices?

Our juices could be kept refrigerated for up to two weeks. If stored in a freezer they can be kept more tha a month.

Do your juices contain additives or preservatives?

No, our juices are 100% natural and contain no additives or preservatives.

Can I order in bulk?

Yes, we offer bulk ordering options for businesses, events, and individuals wishing to purchase in large quantities. Please contact us for a personalized quote.

Do you run promotions or special offers?
Yes, we regularly run promotions and special offers. Just drop by the store and send us a photo of your purchase. You'll get a 20% discount on your order.
Our promotions may vary. Visit our website regularly to be informed of our latest promotions.
Do your juices contain no added sugar?

No, our juices contain sugar by default.
However, we do accept custom orders for diet and no-sugar versions. Please contact us for your customs orders.

About Enerjus

At Enerjus, we're passionate about bringing the vibrant flavors of Africa to your glass. Our commitment to quality and freshness is unmatched, as we source only the finest, sun-ripened fruits from high quality providers.

What sets us apart is our dedication to preserving the natural goodness of our ingredients. We use innovative cold-press technology to extract maximum flavor and nutrients, ensuring that every sip of Enerjus is packed with the benefits nature intended.

Our juices not only tantalize your taste buds but also contribute to your overall well-being. Experience the power of African fruits and discover a new world of refreshment with Enerjus.
